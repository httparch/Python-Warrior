The portal is right behind you. <br><br>
Use <span style="color: green;">Player.setDir("RIGHT") or Player.setDir("LEFT")</span> to change direction.<br><br>

<span style="font-style: italic; font-size: 10px; color: #4c4c4c;">Player.setDir("LEFT"/"RIGHT") Changes the direction in which the player faces. This is useful for navigating paths that require turns.</span>
